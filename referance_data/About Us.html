About Our Pediatric Nephrology Unit

Dedicated to providing world-class, compassionate care for children with kidney disease and their families.

volunteer_activism

Our Mission

Our mission is to be a leader in pediatric kidney care through a commitment to excellence in clinical services, innovative research, and comprehensive education for patients, families, and healthcare professionals. We strive to improve the health and well-being of every child we treat, fostering an environment of hope, healing, and support.

history

Our History

Founded in 1985, our unit began as a small clinic with a vision to provide specialized care for children with renal disorders. Over the decades, we have grown into a comprehensive, state-of-the-art facility, consistently pioneering new treatments and contributing to global pediatric nephrology research. Our journey is one of dedication, innovation, and unwavering focus on our young patients.
